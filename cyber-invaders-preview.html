About Us

The NIET Cyber Security Club (CyberInvaders) is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting cybersecurity awareness, education, and skills development. Our mission is to create a community of security-minded individuals who collaborate to tackle evolving cyber threats.

Our Mission

To cultivate a generation of cybersecurity professionals equipped with the knowledge, skills, and ethical foundation needed to protect digital assets and infrastructure in an increasingly connected world.

Activities

CTF Competitions

Participate in Capture The Flag competitions to test and enhance your practical cybersecurity skills.

Workshops

Hands-on sessions on ethical hacking, penetration testing, digital forensics, and more.

Security Research

Collaborative research on emerging vulnerabilities, threat intelligence, and defense mechanisms.

Guest Lectures

Learn from industry experts and cybersecurity professionals through talks and Q&A sessions.

Security Audits

Help secure campus technology infrastructure through authorized security assessments.

Certification Prep

Study groups and resources to prepare for industry certifications like Security+, CEH, and OSCP.
