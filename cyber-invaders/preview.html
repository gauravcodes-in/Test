About Us

The NIET Cyber Security Club "CyberInvaders" is a community of passionate security enthusiasts dedicated to exploring and advancing the field of cybersecurity. Our mission is to cultivate a security-first mindset and develop cutting-edge skills to protect digital infrastructure against evolving threats.

Mission

To create a collaborative environment where students can explore, learn, and excel in cybersecurity through hands-on activities, knowledge sharing, and industry connections.
