About Us

The NIET Cyber Security Club is dedicated to fostering cybersecurity awareness, skills development, and community engagement.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical skills in cybersecurity through workshops, capture the flag competitions, security research, and industry connections. By creating a vibrant community of like-minded individuals, we prepare students for the ever-evolving landscape of digital security.
